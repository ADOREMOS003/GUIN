Summary

I am a Graph ML and computational biology researcher with experience building heterogeneous knowledge graphs, graph neural network models, and reproducible machine learning pipelines for therapeutic target discovery, drug repurposing, and translational biomarker research.

I have built multi-omics graph workflows spanning 15+ biomedical databases, applied PyTorch-based graph learning methods to prioritize compounds and genes across complex diseases, with emphasis in neurodegenerative and neuropsychiatric disorders, and developed real-world data pipelines over electronic health records and genotype arrays for 400,000+ participants in the All of Us Research Program.

  • Developed a signed GNN model to trace cellular signatures of 3,000+ complex traits and diseases by linking PerturbSeq and UK Biobank signals.
  • Engineered EHR and genotype-array data pipelines for 400,000+ All of Us participants (10+ PB) to support stratification and phenotype discovery.
  • Built reproducible Python, Bash, Docker, and HPC/cloud workflows across transcriptomics, multimodal neuroimaging, and consortium-scale datasets.
